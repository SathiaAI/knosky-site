Trust & privacy

Local-first by design. No KnoSky hosted body-store of your source in Wave 1.

What leaves the machine

  • Default path: map stays local; no always-on product telemetry (DEC-114).
  • You choose when to share a city/artifact — review first.
  • Windows: doctor must not be used to claim kernel no-egress until packaging proves it.

Claim ceiling

Only promise what knosky doctor shows on that install. Having multi-helper (L3) pieces installed does not mean “several AIs are fully safe everywhere by default.”
