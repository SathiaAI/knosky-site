Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

KnoSky is a free, local-first developer tool. This policy covers the knosky.com website. The tool itself runs on your own machine and never sends your code or files to us or anyone else.

What the website collects

If you enter your email address on this site, we store it so we can send you the link to KnoSky and the occasional note about the project. That is the only personal information the website collects. We do not collect your name, and the site uses no third-party advertising or behavioral analytics trackers.

How we use it

  • To send you the KnoSky link you requested.
  • To occasionally email you updates about KnoSky. You can unsubscribe at any time from the link in any email.

Who processes it

Email addresses are collected and stored through Kit (ConvertKit), our email service provider, subject to their privacy terms. We do not sell, rent, or trade your email address.

Your choices

You can unsubscribe at any time, and you can ask us to delete your email from our list. To make a request, email us at sathia@sathia.ai.

The tool itself

KnoSky runs entirely on your machine. It indexes pointers and short excerpts of your own files (never full file bodies) and does not upload your source. Nothing the tool processes is sent to us.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the date above.
